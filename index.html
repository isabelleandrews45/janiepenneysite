about



Greetings. I'm Janie Penney. My real name is Jane but no one calls me that except my sister and my dentist. I am a self-proclaimed writer, photographer, editor, cinematographer, director, designer, producer, and beyond. There should be a word for all that. If there is and I don't know of it, please reach out to me personally.

Currently working in advertising by day, but constantly searching for the most intellectually and aesthetically pleasing aspects of our universe, and finding the right outlet in which I can share them with those willing to listen. I hope that this platform will allow me to reach a wider audience because I think my mom is sick of being my only fan. Be groovy or leave, man.

Cheers!
